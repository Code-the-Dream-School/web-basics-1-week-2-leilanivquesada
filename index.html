Who am I?

My name is Leilani Quesada, and I am a Web Developer and business consultant based out of the San Francisco Bay Area. I am an avid volunteer, a lifelong learner, and a meticulous worker. My biggest career goal is to work in service to society, to help non profit organizations to expand their reach, obtain necessary funding and optimize their processes.

In my free time, l enjoy gardening, crocheting and knitting, taking dance classes, traveling, and reading.

I have a passion for bringing things online! Let’s make a website for your organization!
